What to Expect at Your Visit

New Patients

Please arrive 10–15 minutes early to complete your new patient forms. Bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and a list of any current medications.

Insurance & Payment

We accept most major dental insurance plans. We'll verify your coverage before your appointment begins so there are no surprises.

Reminders

You will receive confirmation and automated reminders from our clinic system. For Macedonian-speaking patients, messages can be sent in Macedonian.

Cancellation Policy

Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance so we can offer the slot to other patients. Repeated no-shows may incur a fee.
